About Me

My name is Saeed Altaf, and I was born in the UK on March 1, 1989. Since, I have had the privelage to live in many different parts of the world, including Munich, Barcelona, Abu Dhabi, Toronto, Austin, Dallas and Atlanta. I feel like being exposed to many different cultures helped me gain interest in a variety of different careers.

During my Bachelor's Degree in Accounting and Information Management (from the University of Texas at Dallas), I undertook an international audit internship at Ernst & Young in Abu Dhabi. Since, I have been able to gain experience in many different industries as an accountant. Upon graduation, I worked as a staff accountant, a property accountant, a fixed asset accountant, and a construction accountant. During these jobs, I was able to utilize the "Information Management" portion of my Bachelor's Degree in order to help optimize reporting and streamline procedures. This was a key for me to move up the ladder to become a Senior Level Accountant.

After my final Senior Accountant position at Cortland Partners, I left the corporate world. I decided to take a leave from working and clear my mind, and re-focus my mind. During my year off, I travelled all over Europe with my wife. Also, during this time, I realized that I want to start a small business, and after doing research and speaking to a relative in the industry, I decided that logistics would be ideal. However, in order for me to learn the industry, I decided I needed to start from the bottom. So, I went out, got my Commercial Driver's License, and became a truck driver! After one year of driving over the road, I got my TWIC card and went into transporting cars. About one year in, I purchased my first truck, and started Grand Trunk Express! I drove for a few months before hiring my first driver and since then, I have grown my fleet to 4 trucks and 4 drivers, all managed by a freight broker.

After starting Grand Trunk Express, and having the business run profitably with minimal intervention on my part, I decided to pursue my passion for programming and technical work. I enrolled in the Georgia Tech Full-Stack Web Development Program, and so far I am loving it! I am hoping to make this the next and final step in my career, and am looking forward to many years in this line of work, as a software developer or web developer!

Oh, and as you can tell, I'm a huge Arsenal fan! Come on you Gunners!
